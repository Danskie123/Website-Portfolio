Problem

The COVID-19 pandemic highlighted the risks associated with physical contact in office environments. Traditional methods of document and package delivery posed a potential risk of virus transmission.

Solution

To address the risks of COVID-19 transmission in offices, we developed an autonomous delivery robot. This robot can navigate safely, disinfect itself with UV light, and deliver items to specific rooms using RFID technology. A mobile app allows users to request deliveries and track the robot's progress. This solution reduces human contact and promotes a safer workplace.

Get Started

The user will be greeted with a startup page containing instructions on how to operate the robot via the app.

Home

After the startup page, the user will be directed to the home page. Here, they can input the room number and select one of three containers to designate the robot's delivery destination.

Outcome

We successfully defended our thesis on August 22, 2024, receiving a grade of 91 out of 100. This experience significantly improved my skills and ability to work under pressure as we completed and presented our autonomous delivery robot.

Overview

Log In

Upon initial launch, users are presented with a login screen. Here, they can input their email address and password. Successful authentication grants access to the home page.

Home

The home page provides a clear overview of the user's medication intake history, displaying the time and date of each entry. A convenient search bar is located at the top of the app, allowing users to access a calendar view. By selecting a specific date, they can easily view their medication intake for that particular day.
